Cold Calling Leads for a Digital Marketing Company


Project Information

  • Category: Cold Calling and Appointment Setting
  • Client: Digital Marketing Agency
  • Project Date: June 2024

I utilized my expertise as a Cold Caller and Appointment Setter to connect window cleaning businesses with a top-notch advertising agency. My role involved reaching out to potential clients, introducing them to the benefits of high-quality lead generation through Facebook and Instagram ads, and setting appointments to discuss their growth opportunities. By presenting a compelling offer and addressing potential concerns, I facilitated meaningful connections and set appointments that led to increased bookings and customer growth for these businesses.

Impact:

  • Engaged with business owners to highlight the effectiveness of exclusive lead generation.
  • Offered a 7-day trial to showcase our strategy.
  • Increased the customer base for window cleaning companies and strengthened their market presence.
